Tips to Identify Genuine Pharmacies

Not all local medical stores maintain the extremely rigorous standards required for pharmaceutical storage. When selecting a trusted pharmacy Kochi, consider these critical factors:

  • Qualified Pharmacists on Duty: A genuine pharmacy will always have a licensed professional available to explain dosages and severe side effects.
  • Proper Facility Infrastructure: Check for functioning refrigerators for specific medicines like insulin drops. Temperature variance ruins drug efficacy!
  • Transparent Sourcing: Genuine pharmacies proudly source their complete inventory from verified distributors.
